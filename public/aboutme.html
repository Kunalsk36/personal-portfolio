About Me

Hello! I'm Kunal Shrikant Kavathekar

I am a passionate and results-driven professional with expertise in Robotic Process Automation, UI/UX design, Android development, and web development. My journey in technology began in college with a simple HTML and CSS website, sparking my passion for continuous exploration and innovation.

Motivated by technology's evolution, I thrive on solving complex problems and creating innovative solutions. My skills enable me to develop efficient workflows, user-friendly interfaces, versatile mobile apps, and dynamic websites. A highlight of my career is developing KKNOTES BSCCS an Android app that provides students with easy access to study materials. The positive feedback and impact reinforced my commitment to user-centric design.

I believe in a user-centric approach, continuous learning, and adaptability to stay ahead in the ever-evolving tech landscape. My work philosophy revolves around understanding user needs and delivering solutions that not only meet but exceed expectations.

For more information or to discuss potential collaborations, please feel free to contact me. I look forward to connecting with you!
